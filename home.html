About Us

NxtEng Canada is a youth-led organization based in Toronto, dedicated to empowering the next generation of global leaders in engineering.

Engineering, a unique and innovative profession, is integral to the development of today's society. Engineers solve problems in healthcare, the environment, transportation, sustainability, and energy. Combining science with design, at its core, engineering is the advancement of humanity.

We want to bring these opportunities to Canadian high school students.
